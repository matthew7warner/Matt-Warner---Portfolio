Project Scope & Challenges

This project required creating a comprehensive learning ecosystem that met strict Texas state regulatory requirements for home inspector licensing. Each course needed to cover specific competencies while maintaining engaging, effective instruction delivery.

Solution & Implementation

I developed a multi-faceted approach combining instructional design best practices with regulatory compliance requirements. Using Adobe Captivate as the primary authoring tool, I created interactive, multimedia-rich courses hosted on TalentLMS, with all supporting materials developed using the Adobe Creative Suite.

Results & Impact

Successfully achieved full state approval for all six courses, meeting 100% of regulatory requirements. The school launched on schedule and provides a complete pathway for students to obtain their Texas home inspector license.
